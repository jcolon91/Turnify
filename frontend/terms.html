2. Description of the service

Bukéame is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform that provides tools for service businesses (barbershops, salons, spas, aesthetic studios, and similar) and their customers to interact. The Platform includes, among others, the following features:

  • For Businesses: scheduling and appointment management, services, staff, customer base, loyalty program, an online product store, waitlist, orders, hours and blocks, basic bookkeeping, public profile, payment-method configuration, referral program, and management of their account and subscription.
