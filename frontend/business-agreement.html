  • To use the data lawfully, inform your customers, and respond to their requests for access, correction, or deletion.

7.3 Bukéame's obligations (processor)

  • Upon termination of the account, to delete or anonymize the data in accordance with the Privacy Policy, except for transaction records, which are retained in anonymized form due to tax requirements of Puerto Rico and the U.S.
